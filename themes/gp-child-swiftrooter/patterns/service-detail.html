Common Questions

How long does a local residential apartment move take in OKC?

Most local moves within Oklahoma City take 3-5 hours for a studio or 1-bedroom, 4-6 hours for a 2-bedroom, and 6-8 hours for a 3-bedroom or larger home. The exact time depends on factors like distance, stairs, and how much you've pre-packed.

Do you disassemble and reassemble furniture?

Yes! Our team will disassemble beds, tables, and other furniture as needed at no extra charge. We'll also reassemble everything at your new location.

What areas of Oklahoma City do you serve?

We serve all of Oklahoma City and surrounding areas within a 75-mile radius, including Edmond, Norman, Moore, Yukon, and Midwest City.

Do you provide moving supplies and boxes?

Yes! We offer moving supplies including boxes, tape, bubble wrap, and packing materials. We can deliver these to you ahead of time or provide full packing services.
